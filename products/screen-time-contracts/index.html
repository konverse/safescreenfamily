Why Contracts Work Better Than Rules

Rules are imposed. Contracts are agreed upon. When kids help shape the agreement, they’re far more likely to follow it – and far less likely to see you as the bad guy.

Every contract in this bundle is designed to be filled out together as a family activity. Kids feel heard. Parents feel confident. Everyone knows where they stand.
